# Privacy Policy

**IfTogether** (hereinafter referred to as the "Service") values the user's personal information and complies with relevant laws and regulations, including the "Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection".
Through this Privacy Policy, the Service informs you of the purpose and method of using the personal information you provide and the measures taken for personal information protection.

This policy is effective from January 10, 2026.

## 1. Personal Information Items Collected and Collection Method

The Service collects the following personal information for membership registration, consultation, and service application.

### A. Collected Personal Information Items
*   **Required Items**: Email address, Login ID (Firebase Auth UID), Nickname, Profile Image URL
*   **Information generated/collected during service use**:
    *   Service usage records, access logs, bad usage records
    *   Device information (Device model, OS version, Device unique identifier, etc.)
    *   Plan information entered by the user, partner connection information, cheer/reaction messages

### B. Collection Method
*   Application execution and membership registration
*   Collection of generated information during service use (Plan creation, Partner connection, etc.)

## 2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Service uses the collected personal information for the following purposes.

*   **Fulfillment of contract for service provision and provision of content according to service provision**: Connection between users (Couple/Partner), Plan sharing, Sending notifications
*   **Member Management**: Identification based on membership service use, personal identification, confirmation of intent to join, handling of complaints, etc.
*   **Use for new service development and marketing/advertising**: Development of new services and provision of customized services, provision of services and advertisements according to demographic characteristics, validation of services, identification of access frequency, statistics on members' service use

## 3. Retention and Usage Period of Personal Information

In principle, after the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved, the information is destroyed without delay. However, the following information is retained for the specified period for the following reasons.

*   **Retained Items**: Login ID, Email, Nickname, Service usage records
*   **Retention Basis**: Until the request for withdrawal of the member or termination of the service
*   **Retention Period**: Destroyed without delay upon membership withdrawal (However, if an investigation or inquiry is in progress due to violation of relevant laws, until the end of such investigation or inquiry)

## 4. Procedure and Method of Destruction of Personal Information

In principle, the Service destroys personal information without delay after the purpose of collection and use is achieved. The procedure and method of destruction are as follows.

*   **Destruction Procedure**: Information entered by the user for membership registration, etc., is transferred to a separate DB (or a separate file box in the case of paper) after the purpose is achieved and destroyed after being stored for a certain period according to internal policies and other relevant laws regarding information protection (refer to Retention and Usage Period).
*   **Destruction Method**: Personal information stored in the form of electronic files is deleted using technical methods that cannot reproduce the record.

## 5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Service generally does not provide users' personal information to the outside. However, exceptions are made in the following cases.

*   When users have consented in advance
*   When there is a request from an investigation agency in accordance with the procedures and methods prescribed by laws for investigation purposes, or in accordance with the provisions of laws
*   **Recipient**: Google LLC (Firebase)
    *   **Purpose of Provision**: Cloud infrastructure operation, database hosting, authentication service
    *   **Provided Items**: Service usage records, email, profile information, generated content

## 6. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users can view or modify their registered personal information at any time and request cancellation of subscription.
*   Viewing/modifying personal information is possible through the [Settings] > [Edit Profile] menu within the application.
*   Cancellation of subscription (withdrawal of consent) is possible through [Settings] > [Delete Account] within the application.
*   Or, if you contact the Privacy Protection Officer by email, we will take action without delay.

## 7. Matters Concerning the Installation, Operation, and Refusal of Automatic Personal Information Collection Devices

The Service operates 'cookies' that store and find user information from time to time. Cookies are very small text files sent to the user's browser by the server used to operate the company's website and are stored on the user's computer hard disk. (In the case of mobile apps, device identifiers, etc., are used.)

*   **Installation/Operation and Refusal**: Users have the option to install cookies. Users can choose to allow or refuse cookies by setting options in their web browser or mobile device settings.
