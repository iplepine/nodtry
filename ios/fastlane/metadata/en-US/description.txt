Struggling to keep a study habit, exam prep, or language routine alive on your own? Nodtry is a study challenge app where you check in on small daily promises with a partner.

Whether it's TOEIC, TOEFL, coding practice, or reading, daily learning routines stick longer when a partner sees them. Nodtry connects today's action, partner confirmations, encouragement, knock-knock reminders, and rewards or penalties into one clear loop.

Great for

- Learners preparing for TOEIC, TOEFL, or other exams a little each day
- Anyone who wants to keep an English, reading, or coding routine for 4+ weeks
- People whose solo study planner keeps fizzling out
- Study buddies and friends who want to motivate each other through check-ins

Key features

- 1:1 partner connection
Connect with a study buddy, friend, or family member through an invite code and keep each other aware of progress.

- Repeatable promises and routines
Set small actions for study, workouts, daily care, recovery, or chores with days, counts, and reminder times.

- Now tab
Handle today's study task, delayed actions, completed actions, and partner status from one focused screen.

- Knock-knock reminders
Send a knock-knock when your partner is quiet or a promise has stopped moving. Missed actions are pulled back into view instead of disappearing silently.

- Check and cheer
When your partner reports a study check-in, confirm it and send a short cheer message or reaction.

- Rewards and penalties
Agree on a reward for success and a penalty for missed commitments, so motivation stays sharp before the week gets busy.

Nodtry is not another to-do list. It is built to keep postponed study promises alive inside a real relationship with a partner.
