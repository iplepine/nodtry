Nodtry is a mutual accountability app for keeping small promises and routines with a partner.

Personal to-do and routine apps are easy to ignore. Nodtry connects your promise to a real relationship, so today's action, partner confirmations, encouragement, knock-knock reminders, and rewards or penalties stay in one clear loop.

Key features

- 1:1 partner connection
Connect with someone close through an invite code and keep each other aware of progress.

- Repeatable promises and routines
Set small actions for workouts, daily care, recovery, chores, or custom routines with days, counts, and reminder times.

- Now tab
Handle today's action, delayed actions, completed actions, and partner status from one focused screen.

- Knock-knock reminders
Send a knock-knock when your partner is quiet or a promise has stopped moving. Missed actions can be pulled back into view instead of disappearing silently.

- Check and cheer
When your partner reports an action, confirm it and send a short cheer message or reaction.

- Rewards and penalties
Agree on a reward for success and a penalty for missed commitments, so motivation is clear before the week gets busy.

Nodtry is not another to-do list. It is built to keep postponed promises alive inside a real relationship.
